About AQI Predictor

Smarter air quality awareness for Sri Lanka

AQI Predictor is a machine-learning-powered web application designed to estimate air quality levels across Sri Lankan cities using pollutant data. It aims to make air quality information easier to understand, more visual, and more useful for day-to-day decision making.
